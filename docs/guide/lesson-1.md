## üé§ Pronunciation Guide (Talaffuz bo'yicha qo'llanma)

### Salom berish (Greeting)

1. **Hello** - Assalomu alaikum yoki eng tilidagi rasmiy salom
   - Audio: HEL-oh
   - Misol: "Hello, how are you?" (Salom, siz qanday?)

2. **Hi** - Norasmiy, do'stona salom
   - Audio: HAI
   - Misol: "Hi there!" (Salom do'st!)

---

## üí¨ Grammar (Grammatika)

### Simple Greetings (Oddiy salamlar)

Eng tilidagi eng sodda salamlar:

```
Hello!
Hi!
Hello, John!
Hi, how are you?
```

### "How are you?" (Siz qanday?)

**Strukturasi:**
- How = Qanday
- are = tu (bo'ish felining hozirgi zamon shakli)
- you = siz

**Javoblar:**
- I'm fine. (Men yaxshi)
- I'm very well. (Men juda yaxshi)
- I'm okay. (Men normal)

## üìñ Examples (Misollar)

### Suhbat 1: Rasmiy salom

```
Person A: Good morning. My name is Ibrahim.
          (Ertalab xayr. Mening ismim Ibrahim.)

Person B: Good morning. Nice to meet you.
          (Ertalab xayr. Siz bilan tanishib faxri.)

Person A: Thank you. How are you?
          (Raxmat. Siz qanday?)

Person B: I'm fine, thank you. And you?
          (Men yaxshi, raxmat. Siz?)

Person A: I'm very well, thank you.
          (Men juda yaxshi, raxmat.)
```

### Suhbat 2: Norasmiy salom

```
Person A: Hi! How's it going?
          (Salom! Nima gaplar?)

Person B: Hey! I'm doing great!
          (Salom! Juda yaxshi!)

Person A: That's awesome! Let's grab coffee.
          (Bu ajoyib! Kofe ichamiz.)

Person B: Sure, let's go!
          (Ha, boramiz!)
```
